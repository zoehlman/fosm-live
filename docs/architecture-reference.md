## Three-Layer Architecture

### Layer 1 — The Vision
The Plan To Heal The World sits above everything else.

### Layer 2 — The Brand Doorways (6 doors, 1 message)
- **Learn And Grow Rich** — parent brand · the warm teacher
  - Houses: MBA Program (project), Beyond The Money (program)
- **Beyond The Money** — program under LGR · the peer who's been there
- **The Foundation by LGR** — sub-brand · the steward
- **The Retreat by LGR** — sub-brand · the guide
- **Money·God·Politics** — parallel brand · the humble student
- **Indiana Oehlman** — personal brand · the friend on the road

### Layer 3 — For-Profit Software
FOSM·LIVE instances for companies. Each instance: M·S·O + L·I·V·E.
- **Apex:** For-Profit Executive CC ("I manage $▢▢▢M across ▢▢ companies")
- **Flagship:** LGR · Learn And Grow Rich
- **Scale target:** 20+ companies

### Layer 4 — Non-Profit Software
FOSM·LIVE instances for foundations.
- "S" pillar = Stewardship (donor cultivation, grants, major gifts)
- "F" pillar = fund accounting (restricted/unrestricted, 990s)
- "M" pillar = cause marketing (impact storytelling, transparency)
- **Apex:** Non-Profit Executive CC
- **Flagship:** Foundation by LGR

### Layer 5 — Retreat Software
FOSM·LIVE instances for healing centers. Each instance tracks candidates
(intake → vetting → pre-medicine → ceremony → integration → alumni) and
cohorts (group dynamics, modality use, outcomes). Extended detail lives
inside each location's "O" pillar.
- **Apex:** Retreat Executive CC
- **Flagship:** Retreat by LGR · The Farm · Colombia

## The Brain — FOSM·LIVE Master

The intelligence layer (NOT an instance). 1 mind · 3 chambers · 4 input streams.

### Three chambers
- **For-Profit Chamber** — companies scaling, L·I·V·E human side, standard FOSM playbooks
- **Non-Profit Chamber** — foundations as businesses, S = Stewardship, fund accounting, 990s
- **Retreat Chamber** — healing centers, candidate & cohort tracking, integration outcomes

### Four input streams
1. **Zach Oehlman** (irreplaceable human intelligence) — Vision · Meditations · Masterminds · Frameworks · Brand voice
2. **Client Data** (field reality) — FOSM KPIs · Financials · Vision boards · L10 outcomes · Audit results · Attribution chains
3. **Industry Data** (sector context) — Sector trends · Benchmarks · Competitor moves · Best practices · Trade research
4. **Economic Data** (macro context) — Macro indicators · Interest rates · Capital markets · Regulatory · Cycles

### Data flow
- ↑ 4 streams → Brain (triangulates all four — gets smarter with every client)
- → Chambers → Instances (each chamber pushes its playbook to matching layer)
- ▲ Instances → 3 Apex CCs (each layer rolls up to its own Executive CC)

## Marketing Command Center (V3 — active focus)

A campaign factory + attribution engine + authority builder. Every brand
(LGR, Foundation, Retreat, MGP, Indiana) plugs in here.

### 9 module groups

1. **Strategy** — Brand & Positioning, ICP/Avatar Library (5 ICPs), Competitor Intel, ★ Campaign Builder Framework (core feature), Quarterly Plan, Annual Plan
2. **Plan & Run** — ★ Master Cross-Brand Calendar, Per-Brand Calendars, Weekly Content Workflow (7 stages), Content Library, Campaign Pipeline, Special Promos
3. **Channels** (15) — LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, X, Facebook+Threads, Email, Podcast, Blog/SEO, Webinar/Live, Paid Ads (variation engine), PR/Media, Speaking, Affiliate Hub
4. **AI Expert Library** — Copywriting, Data Analytics, Campaign Structure, Per-Platform experts, Architecture, Brand Voice Sentinel
5. **Forum / Community Engine** — Source Scrapers, Question Detector, Context Reader, Reply Drafter, ★ Approve/Edit/Deny, Authority Tracker
6. **Outreach & Affiliates** — Finance Influencer, CPA/Accountant Affiliate, Law Firm Partners, PE Demo, Podcast Guesting Engine, Partner Vetting
7. **Funnel & Leads** — Funnel Builder, Lead Pipeline (Cold→MQL→SQL), Webinar Funnels, ★ Handoff to Sales CC
8. **Attribution Engine** (Hyros-style) — ★ Per-Content Tracking, Cross-Channel Stitching, ★ "Got A Client" View (primary lens), Vanity Quarantine, ROI per Content Type, Recommendation Loop
9. **Money & Team** — Marketing Budget, ROI/CAC/LTV, Team/Roles, Agencies/Vendors, FOSM Connection, Integrations

### Handoff to Sales CC
When SQL meets criteria, lead is posted to Sales CC with full attribution chain
· ICP match score · channel of origin · all content consumed · brand voice they
responded to · webinar(s) attended · CTA(s) clicked.

## Executive Command Center (V4 — multi-tenant from day 1)

Three Executive CCs — one per software layer (For-Profit, Non-Profit, Retreat).
Same architecture, different vocabulary.

### Three tenant types (from day 1)
- **Tenant Type A** — You (LGR portfolio) · LIVE day 1
- **Tenant Type B** — PE / Family Office · licensee architecture day 1
- **Tenant Type C** — Agency / Holdco · licensee architecture day 1

### Portfolio grid
Each entity shown with FOSM·LIVE status (on track / ops red / all green / leader
struggling / sales tweak). Roll-up: Gross Revenue · Gross Profit · Net Income ·
Assets · Liabilities · Cash Flow.

### Summary screen per entity
Mission/vision/values · top 3 wins · top 3 risks · what to listen for next call
· pillar trends · open rocks · cash position · last week's marketing/sales delta
· deep-link button to full FOSM·LIVE.

**"Hide private details" toggle** — replaces client names with Client A/B for
screen-sharing demos. All numbers and statuses preserved.

### Architecture notes
- Tenant isolation by design (row-level security at DB layer, not app layer)
- Shared schema, separate data (one upgrade benefits all tenants)
- Roles: tenant admin · entity owner · entity contributor · read-only viewer

### The 7-question voice check (every post)
1. Sounds like Zach, not a generic marketer?
2. Inquiry-led, not declaration-led?
3. Avoids moralizing?
4. Gives value before asking?

## Build Order (V7 — recommended sequence)

Engineered for maximum compounding. Each step accelerates the next.

1. ✅ **System map** — wireframe v0.1 (V1–V8 in repo, V1–V7 deployed to Netlify)
2. 🔨 **Marketing CC** — back-end architecture + front-end polish. DB schema, multi-tenant ready, Voice Sentinel, attribution engine, calendar sync
3. ⏳ **Sales CC** — pipeline, audit intake, webinar funnels, Triangle of Death. Closes loop on attribution to actual closes
4. ⏳ **Operations CC** — SOP library, L10, owner scorecards, ClickUp sync. Unlocks the multi-tenant licensee story
5. ⏳ **Vision-to-Reality CC** — highest-leverage of the LIVE pillars (sets every goal)
6. ⏳ **Executive CC (multi-tenant launch)** — once Marketing + Sales + Ops + V2R stable for ONE tenant (you)
7. ⏳ **Foundation CC** — build when Foundation has revenue to track
8. ⏳ **Retreat CC** — last because it requires the hardest content

### Open architecture decisions
- **Stack** — Node/Postgres? Python? Supabase?
- **Auth & tenancy** — SSO? Per-tenant subdomains vs shared workspace switcher? RBAC granularity?
- **AI provider** — Anthropic/OpenAI/local? One provider per task or model-routed?
